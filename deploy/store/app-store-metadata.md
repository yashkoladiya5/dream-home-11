## Full Description (4000 chars max)

**What is Dream Home 11?**

Dream Home 11 is India's first fantasy home contest platform where your knowledge of real estate, interiors, and home design can win you actual homes. Predict contest outcomes, compete on leaderboards, and earn points that bring you closer to your dream home.

**How It Works**

1. **Join a Contest** — Browse daily home-themed contests covering property prices, interior design trends, renovation costs, and real estate market predictions.
2. **Make Your Predictions** — Use your knowledge and insights to predict contest outcomes. The more accurate your predictions, the more points you earn.
3. **Climb the Leaderboard** — Compete against thousands of players. Top-ranked contestants win big prizes including homes, gold, electronics, and cash.
4. **Withdraw Your Winnings** — KYC-verified withdrawals with fast processing. Real homes are awarded to qualified winners with full legal title transfer.

**Key Features**

- 🏠 Daily home & real estate contests
- 📊 Real-time leaderboards with live rankings
- 💰 Real homes, cash, and rewards as prizes
- 🎯 Multiple contest types: head-to-head, mega contests, private leagues
- 👥 Community features: chat, feed, friend invites
- 🔐 KYC-verified secure withdrawals
- 📱 Available in English and Hindi
- 🎮 Spin-to-win and streak bonuses
- 🏆 Achievement badges and milestone rewards
- 📞 24/7 customer support

**Why Users Love Dream Home 11**

- **Real Prizes**: Actual homes awarded to verified winners with complete legal documentation
- **Skill-Based**: Success depends on knowledge and strategy, making every win truly earned
- **Transparent**: All contests, winners, and prize distributions are publicly verifiable
